Ingredients:
 - 1 all beef patty
 - 1 sesame bun
 - 1 slice of american cheese
 - 1 slice of cheddar cheese
 - 4 spicy pickles
 - 1 oz of ketchup
 - Salt


Directions:
 1. Preheat the grill.
 
 2. Put the burger on the grill and add salt on top. Cook for 5 min and flip.
 
 3. After 3 min add cheese. Meanwhile toss the bun on the top rack for 2 min.

 4. Add pickles and condiments.
